Elevating Equine Performance:
HBA’s Comprehensive Solutions

HBA Product Offerings:

HBA Climate Designed Supplements: At the pinnacle of equine nutrition, our supplements are meticulously crafted to excel in extreme conditions. With centuries of Arabian equestrian expertise, HBA offers products tailored for high-performance horses, adhering to ISO 9001:2008 standards. Proven to enhance and maintain peak condition, our supplements are trusted globally by equestrian professionals.

Innovative Research and Real-World Testing: Based in Dubai, where temperatures can reach 50ºC and endurance races cover up to 160 kilometres, we conduct rigorous in-house testing. Founder Hassan bin Ali's extensive experience drives our commitment to optimizing equine performance through practical, innovative research.
